PauloVM FAQ

- What is PauloVM?
PauloVM is a fork of CollabVM. We are a project where you can control virtual machines online with friends or other people.
- Why did I get banned?
Because you broke the PauloVM Site Guidelines. You are required to read them before using the site.
- Who owns PauloVM?
XPigeon owns PauloVM and is the founder of the project.
- How to apply for Pidgeys?
You can apply for Pidgeys if we announce it.
- When do we get pinged?
When something important has happened. Most of the time, we use reaction pings if it isn't important.
- When do I get banned?
You get banned when we see your being annoying or when your a threat for core functions of PauloVM.
- What if I see a "Pidove" or a "Pidgey" abuse?
Make a ticket in the discord Server and report the person abusing. Give info about the person and screenshots where is shown that the person is abusing.
